About us

TAQIA_RESTO is where tradition meets modernity. We take pride in offering our guests the best of Kazakh cuisine, prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From the hearty beshbarmak to the delicate baursaks, each dish reflects the warmth and comfort of a true Kazakh dastarkhan.Our chefs craft dishes inspired by ancient recipes, preserving the unique tastes and aromas of the steppe. Join us to experience the atmosphere of a true Kazakh feast in the heart of the city.
